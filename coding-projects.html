Reverse Horoscope
I do not believe in Astrology. However, I do believe that what pulls many towards Astrology is the act of reading something that seems like it's written for them and deciding whether or not it applies to you. Why not take out the middle man? In my Reverse Horoscope, I take daily horoscopes from other websites, teaching myself how to web scrape with JSoup, and have the user select which body of text applies to them in a two-part quiz, then saying which "sign" they are based on their answers.

Scoobie Cipher
Based on an assignment to make a simple cipher in my Object Oriented Programming course, I was hit with inspiration. What if the encrypted text of a message could be translated further into something delightful: perhaps something kin to the acclaimed nonsense of, say, Scatman John ? Thus, the Scoobie Cipher was born. A shadow of Scoobie Cipher's potential was submitted for credit, in the interest of prioritizing my school work, but it will reach its true glory. Soon.
