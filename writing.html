Scuba Girl
"Scuba Girl" is a flash fiction that I published on Daily Science Fiction. I wrote it after spending 14 hours over the course of two days trying to edit fiction that would go on to be much less successful. Its success was a strange mix between enjoying the miracle of writing a decent story by pure inspiration, and respect for the hours of hard work that brought me to that point.
